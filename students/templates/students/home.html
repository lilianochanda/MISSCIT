SCIT PROJECT MANAGEMENT

This platform helps final year students and their supervisors interact easily as they monitor their projects and give feedback.

About Us

SCIT student project managemet system is a platform where students upload their projects and submit them to their supervisors. The supervisor is able to download the project and check whether the student has done it as required. If not the supervisor gives a remark that is sent back to the student for correction. After the three phases of submission of the project the final copy of the project is sent to the coordinating supervisor.
